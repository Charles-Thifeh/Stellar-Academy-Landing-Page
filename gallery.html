Entrance Assessment
What to Expect

Our entrance assessment is designed to help us understand each child's strengths, learning style, and readiness — not to exclude, but to ensure every scholar is placed in the right year group to thrive.

1
Submit Application

Complete the online application form and submit the required documents including birth certificate, immunisation record, and previous school reports.

2
Invitation to Assess

Shortlisted applicants are invited for a friendly, informal assessment session held on campus. Parents/guardians are welcome to attend.

3
The Assessment Day

Children participate in age-appropriate literacy, numeracy, and oral activities. Our pastoral team also conducts a brief parent interview to understand the family's expectations.

4
Offer & Enrolment

Successful applicants receive a formal offer letter within 5 working days. Upon acceptance and payment of the enrolment fee, your scholar's place is secured.
