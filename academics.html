Early Childhood

At Stellar Scholars Academy, we believe the early years are the most critical window for fostering a lifelong love of discovery. Our Early Stages program is designed to transform natural curiosity into a structured foundation for academic excellence.

Our Philosophy: "Wonder Meet Wisdom"
We don't just teach children what to learn; we teach them how to explore. Our curriculum balances play-based discovery with the essential building blocks of literacy and numeracy. We create a "brave space" where mistakes are celebrated as milestones and every "why?" is met with an opportunity to find out.

  • Manipulative-based mathematics with problem-solving focus

Middle & High School

The Secondary Education division at Evergreen School is a holistic and rigorous environment intentionally designed to meet the unique developmental journey of the adolescent learner—from early adolescence through graduation. We are committed to nurturing the whole student, guiding them through identity formation, fostering intellectual curiosity, and developing the ethical character essential for future leadership. Our goal is to empower every student to pursue their path of learning, growing, and contributing as a world-class citizen. Our comprehensive curriculum blends the essential rigor of the Nigerian national curriculum with the globally recognized British framework, ensuring our students are prepared for success on both local and international stages. This dynamic academic foundation, featuring core subjects and varied electives, directly supports our Mission: To equip learners with essential skills through a Christ-centered, globally relevant education that fosters a passion for lifelong learning, ethical character, and innovation. Through our specialized advisory program, every student benefits from an adult advocate who provides continuous mentorship, navigating academic choices and emotional growth. By instilling these Christ-centered values and demanding excellence, the Secondary Division actively progresses toward our Vision: To be the top educational institution for developing world-class leaders who are rooted in Christ and skilled in all wisdom.
