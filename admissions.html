How It Works

The Admissions Process

A clear, straightforward journey from enquiry to enrolment — we're with you every step of the way.

1
📋

Enquiry

Complete our online enquiry form or visit the school to express interest and receive our prospectus.

2
📝

Application

Submit the completed application form along with required documents and the ₦5,000 application fee.

3
✏️

Assessment

Your child attends a friendly entrance assessment — a relaxed session to gauge readiness and learning style.

4
🤝

Interview

A brief parent-and-child meeting with the Principal — an opportunity to ask questions and tour the campus.

5
🎉

Enrolment

Receive your offer letter, pay the acceptance deposit, and welcome to the Stellar Scholars family!

Entry Requirements

What You'll Need to Apply

Each division has tailored entry criteria to ensure every scholar is placed in the right environment to thrive. Below are the standard requirements across all divisions.

Applications are considered on a rolling basis. We encourage families to apply early as spaces are limited. Siblings of current scholars receive priority consideration.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦
Sibling Priority Policy

Siblings of currently enrolled scholars receive priority placement and a 10% sibling discount on tuition fees. Please indicate sibling status on your application form.

🌱
Pre-School
Ages 3 – 5 · Nursery & Reception
  • ✓ Birth certificate (original + copy)
  • ✓ Immunisation records / health card
  • ✓ 4 passport photographs of child
  • ✓ Completed application form + ₦5,000 fee
📚
Grade School
Ages 6 – 11 · Grade 1 – 6
  • ✓ Birth certificate + immunisation records
  • ✓ Previous school report cards (last 2 terms)
  • ✓ Entrance assessment (Literacy & Numeracy)
  • ✓ School transfer letter (if applicable)
🔭
Junior Secondary (JSS)
Ages 11 – 14 · JSS 1 – 3
  • ✓ Grade 6 leaving certificate / FSLC
  • ✓ Recent academic report cards
  • ✓ Written entrance exam (core subjects)
  • ✓ Parent-Principal interview
🎓
Senior Secondary (SSS)
Ages 15 – 17 · SSS 1 – 3
  • ✓ JSS 3 certificate / BECE result
  • ✓ Comprehensive entrance exam + oral interview
  • ✓ Statement of academic goals (student-written)
  • ✓ Previous school reference letter
