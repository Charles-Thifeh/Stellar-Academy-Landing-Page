Find Us

Our Location

Stellar Scholars Academy
Off Lagos - Ibadan Expy, Ojora Street, Orimerunmu, Sagamu 110113, Ogun State
Open in Google Maps Open in Waze
🚗 By Car

From Kaduna city centre, take the Barnawa Road heading south. Turn onto Quarantine Dam Road — the school gate is on your right, marked by the blue SSA signboard.

🚌 By Bus / Keke

Board any bus or keke-napep heading to Anguwan Tasha from Barnawa Junction. Alight at Quarantine Dam bus stop — the school is a 3-minute walk.

🅿️ Parking

Free parking is available within the school compound. Visitor parking is clearly signposted to the left of the main gate. Security will direct you on arrival.
